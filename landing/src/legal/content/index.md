# Legal

Last updated: May 15, 2026.

These pages describe the baseline terms, privacy practices, and data processing commitments for Inline. They apply to Inline's websites, apps, APIs, SDKs, bot surfaces, MCP server, and related services unless a separate signed agreement says otherwise.

| Document | What it covers |
| --- | --- |
| [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy) | Personal data we collect, how we use it, how we share it, and the choices available to users. |
| [Terms of Service](/legal/terms) | The rules for using Inline, including accounts, content, integrations, service availability, and liability. |
| [Acceptable Use Policy](/legal/aup) | Conduct and content that are not allowed on Inline. |
| [Subprocessors](/legal/subprocessors) | Third-party service providers used to operate Inline and optional user-enabled integrations. |
| [Data Processing Addendum](/legal/dpa) | Processor terms for customers that use Inline with personal data subject to privacy laws such as GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA, or similar laws. |

Questions about these documents can be sent to [hey@inline.chat](mailto:hey@inline.chat). Security concerns should also include the subject line `Security`.
